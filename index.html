Is AnswerlyAI replacing our receptionists?

No. Think of it as a teammate that handles repetitive calls and after-hours coverage. Your staff still owns complex situations and the human moments that matter.

Which industries do you support first?

We’re focused on service businesses with steady call volume: medical & dental clinics, salons, spas, trades, and local professional services.

What tools can you integrate with?

In early access we’re focusing on calendar tools, basic CRMs, and popular scheduling platforms. Tell us what you use and we’ll confirm compatibility.

How long does onboarding take?

Most teams go from kickoff to first live calls in 1–2 weeks, depending on complexity of scripts and routing rules.
